4-Piece sets are somewhat less restrictive than 2-Piece

Strict LEG: Life / Offense / Defense / Crit Rate / Divine Offense / Divine Crit Rate / Divine Life / Resilience / Fatal
 * Only Legendary pieces.  Primary stats matching set.
SPD Focus: Speed / Divine Speed
 * Pretty loose, looking for speed rolls.  Boots a little more strict.
DAM Focus: 
 * Damage and speed only.
SURVIVE/DAM: Regeneration / Stalwart / Immortal / Guardian / Protection / Defiant
 * Good spread of survivability, accuracy, and damage.  No Atk% damage.
SURVIVE/DAM/ATK: Accuracy / Cursed / Provoke / Perception / Righteous
 * Same as SURVIVE/DAM with ATK% damage.
Everything:
 * Too complex, keeping all >= 6* epic, non flat substats and flat primary stats.
